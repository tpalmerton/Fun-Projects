Unit Conversions
Temperature



To convert celcius to fehrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the `C` with your temperature in celcius: (C x 9/5) + 32
Weight



To convert kilograms to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the `kg` with your weight in kilograms: gr x 2.205
Distance



To convert kilometers to miles yourself, use this formula replacing the `km` with your distance in kilometers: km ÷ 1.609
